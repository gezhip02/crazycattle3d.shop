Frequently Asked Questions

What are the system requirements for Windows?

Minimum requirements:
- Windows 10 64-bit
- Intel Core i5 or equivalent
- 8GB RAM
- DirectX 11 compatible GPU
- 2GB storage space

What are the system requirements for macOS?

Minimum requirements:
- macOS 10.15 or newer
- Apple M1 or Intel Core i5
- 8GB RAM
- Metal compatible GPU
- 2GB storage space

What are the system requirements for Linux?

Minimum requirements:
- Ubuntu 20.04 or equivalent
- Intel Core i5 or equivalent
- 8GB RAM
- OpenGL 4.5 compatible GPU
- 2GB storage space

Is there a multiplayer mode?

Yes! Crazy Cattle 3D features both online multiplayer and local split-screen modes. Online matches support up to 32 players simultaneously, while local split-screen allows 2-4 players on the same device.

How long does a typical match last?

A standard match typically lasts around 30 minutes, but we also offer quick-play modes that last 15 minutes and extended matches that can go up to 45 minutes. You can choose the match duration in the game settings.

Are there different game modes available?

Yes, Crazy Cattle 3D offers several game modes:
- Classic Battle Royale
- Team Deathmatch
- Capture the Flag
- Practice Mode (with AI bots)
- Custom Games (create your own rules)

Is there cross-platform play?

Yes, Crazy Cattle 3D supports cross-platform play between Windows, macOS, and Linux users. Players can join matches regardless of their platform, and our matchmaking system ensures fair competition across all platforms.

How often do you release updates?

We release major updates every three months, introducing new features, maps, and gameplay elements. Bug fixes and minor improvements are released more frequently, typically every 2-3 weeks. All updates are free for all players.
