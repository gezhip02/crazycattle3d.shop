Crazy Cattle 3D - Version 2.4.0 Update Notes

March 18, 2024 6 min read Updates

Welcome to the latest major update for Crazy Cattle 3D! Version 2.4.0 brings exciting new features, gameplay improvements, and balance changes to enhance your battle royale experience. Let's dive into all the changes and additions in this substantial update.

New Features

New Map: New Zealand Highlands

  • Expansive mountain ranges with multiple elevation levels
  • Dense forest areas for stealth gameplay
  • Interactive water features including rivers and lakes
  • Dynamic weather system affecting visibility and movement
  • Unique power-up locations and strategic points

Enhanced Movement System

  • Improved wall-bouncing mechanics for smoother transitions
  • New slide-boost technique for advanced movement
  • Refined air control for better maneuverability
  • Added momentum preservation through terrain transitions

New Game Mode: Team Tactics

  • 4v4 objective-based gameplay
  • Strategic capture points across the map
  • Team-specific power-ups and abilities
  • New scoring system for objective play

Balance Changes

Ability Adjustments

  • Rocket Jump
    • Cooldown reduced from 12s to 10s
    • Self-damage reduced by 15%
    • Knockback increased by 10%
  • Shield Bubble
    • Duration increased to 6s (was 5s)
    • Health reduced by 100 points
    • Now allows projectiles to pass outward
  • Speed Boost
    • Movement speed bonus reduced to 35% (was 40%)
    • Added slight acceleration period
    • Duration increased to 8s (was 6s)

Weapon Balance

  • Wool Launcher
    • Damage falloff starts at longer range
    • Projectile speed increased by 5%
    • Magazine size increased to 6 (was 5)
  • Hay Maker
    • Spread pattern tightened slightly
    • Reload speed increased
    • Damage per pellet adjusted to 8 (was 9)

Quality of Life Improvements

UI/UX Updates

  • New customizable HUD options
  • Improved minimap clarity and zoom options
  • Added ability cooldown indicators
  • New ping system for better team communication
  • Colorblind mode options

Performance Optimization

  • Improved frame rate stability in dense areas
  • Reduced memory usage by ~15%
  • Optimized network code for better latency
  • Enhanced texture streaming system

Bug Fixes

  • Fixed collision issues on Ireland map edges
  • Resolved occasional ability cancellation bug
  • Fixed incorrect score display in Team Mode
  • Addressed rare crash during map transition
  • Fixed audio desync in spectator mode
  • Resolved inventory sorting issues

Known Issues

  • Some weather effects may cause minor FPS drops
  • Rare visual glitch with Shield Bubble in water
  • Occasional delay in team voice chat

We're continuously working to improve Crazy Cattle 3D and appreciate all the feedback from our community. Stay tuned for more updates and hotfixes as we continue to enhance your gaming experience!
